# CLIENT ONBOARDING

## Introduction
Welcome to MM Digital Studio! We are excited to have you as a client. In this document, you will find the onboarding instructions and checklist to ensure a smooth start to our collaboration.

## Client Onboarding Steps

1. **Initial Meeting**  
   Schedule an initial meeting with your project manager to discuss your goals, expectations, and project details.

2. **Project Plan Development**  
   Work with our team to create a project plan that includes timelines, deliverables, and milestones.

3. **Access and Permissions**  
   Provide necessary access to relevant tools and platforms (e.g., project management tools, design software).

4. **Communication Channels**  
   Establish communication channels (e.g., Slack, email) and project management tools to ensure effective collaboration.

5. **Brand Guidelines**  
   Share your brand guidelines and any existing materials that will help us understand your brand and vision.

6. **Feedback Process**  
   Discuss the feedback process to ensure timely and efficient reviews of the work provided by our team.

## Client Onboarding Checklist
- [ ] Initial meeting scheduled
- [ ] Project plan in place
- [ ] Access granted to necessary tools
- [ ] Communication channels established
- [ ] Brand guidelines shared
- [ ] Feedback process agreed upon

## Conclusion
Thank you for choosing MM Digital Studio! We look forward to a successful partnership and are here to support you throughout the process.
